About My Art

I've been drawing Murder Drones characters for over a year now, constantly experimenting with different styles and techniques. My work focuses on capturing the perfect balance between the mechanical nature of the drones and their surprisingly human emotions.

"I don't just draw robots - I give souls to machines and tell stories through circuits and steel."

When I'm not drawing, I'm probably brainstorming my next piece or collaborating with other artists in the community. The Murder Drones fandom has been incredibly supportive, and I love being part of this creative family.

Feel free to check out my girlfriend's art too - she's an amazing artist who inspires me every day!
